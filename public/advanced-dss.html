What is DSS?

The Decision Support System transforms your soil analysis into actionable irrigation recommendations using:

  • FAO-56 Methodology - Global standard for crop water requirements
  • BBCH Growth Stages - Precise crop development tracking
  • Localized Kc Values - Regional climate adjustments
  • Economic Analysis - ROI and cost-benefit calculations
Pro Tip: Ensure your soil analysis is recent (within 6 months) for best results.
