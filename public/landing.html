About FlahaSoil

FlahaSoil is part of the Flaha Precision Agriculture ecosystem, bringing scientific soil analysis to agricultural professionals worldwide. Our platform combines cutting-edge algorithms with intuitive design to make soil analysis accessible and actionable.

Built on proven Saxton & Rawls pedotransfer functions, FlahaSoil provides accurate soil water characteristic calculations that help farmers, consultants, and researchers make informed decisions about crop selection and soil management.
